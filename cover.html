What Is Love ?

By :- Sanyam Rudola

            Love is a complex emotion involving deep affection, attachment, and 
            connection, encompassing romantic, platonic, self, and 
            altruistic forms across different contexts and cultures.


            It is not about what you are getting,
            It is about what you are giving.


            Love is the silent symphony that plays in the heart, resonating with the gentle whispers of the soul,
            and painting the world in colors only two hearts can see.
        
            
                Don't love someone because you think they can sacrifice the most for you,
                Always love someone because you think you can sacrifice the most for them.
