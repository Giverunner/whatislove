Love Languages Revisited

            it opened the door to a series of conversations that would bring back memories and 
            unravel the reasons behind her sudden departure.

            needed to focus on her studies and personal growth.

            but her tone was often judgy and critical of my life choices, 
            including my college and grades.

            but she refused, saying that things had changed too much.

            Over time, we transitioned from being acquaintances to good friends, and finally, 
            she called me her bestie.
