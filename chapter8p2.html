Part - II

            We also found joy in playful banter. Using my knowledge of numerology, 
            I would often tease her, proving why she was my soulmate based on numbers and stars. 
            One day, she shared an intriguing concept with me: twin flames. 
            She explained that twin flames are two souls that are destined to separate and reunite multiple times ,
            until they fully accept and understand each other. This idea resonated deeply with me, and 
            it felt like an explanation for the tumultuous yet profound connection we shared.

            Our bond grew stronger as we navigated this new phase of our relationship. 
            Despite the initial rudeness and judgments, Jyoti gradually softened, and 
            we found a rhythm that worked for us. 
            We learned to accept each other's flaws and celebrated our unique bond.

            The playful nicknames, the deep conversations about our past, and 
            the new memories we created together became the foundation of our rekindled friendship. 
            It wasn't always smooth, but it was genuine, and that's what mattered the most.

            This journey with Jyoti taught me a lot about patience, forgiveness, and the complexities of relationships. 
            It showed me that sometimes, people come back into our lives to teach us lessons, 
            to challenge us, and to help us grow. 
            And while our relationship was not the same as it once was, it was evolving into something new and beautiful.

            Our connection was a testament to the idea that some bonds are meant to endure, 
            no matter how much time passes or how many obstacles come in the way. And so, 
            with hope and a renewed sense of understanding, 
            we continued to explore what the future held for us, one day at a time.
